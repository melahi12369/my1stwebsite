Top 5 Games to try out now!

  1. Bloodborne - This game is one of my favorites. If you like fast-paced action, great lore, and eldritch horrors, this one is for you!
  2. Nioh - Into Japanese culture and mythology? This Souls-Like clone by Team Ninja will school you in Japan's rich history and culture while testing your skills.
  3. Final Fantasy 12 - I am probably one of the biggest Final Fantasy fans you know... Final Fantasy 12 was a great addition to the series, great music, great battles, and a great amount of content!
  4. Kingdom Hearts Series - This series is one to check out if you think Final Fantasy and Disney go together. Don't let that fool you, check this classic out for yourself!
  5. Okami - The last on the list, but far from "worst." If you can't tell by now by this pick, I love Japanese culture. Don't call me a weeb!

That's all I have for this month! Go check out these games now, you'll definitely love them, and some of them will probably make it into your top 5 as well!
